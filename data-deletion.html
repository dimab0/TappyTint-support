Tappy Tint stores progress locally on your device. We do not maintain server-side user accounts or cloud-stored personal data for this app.

How to Delete App Data

  1. Touch and hold the Tappy Tint app icon on your iPhone or iPad.
  2. Tap Remove App.
  3. Tap Delete App to remove the app and all local app data.

Need Help?

If you need help with deletion steps, contact support via GitHub Issues.
