Common Questions

How do I color a region?

Tap any enclosed area. Use pinch gestures to zoom in/out for precise coloring.

How do I undo?

Use the undo button in the coloring screen.

Where is my progress saved?

Progress is stored locally on your device. There is no account system or cloud sync.

How do I reset progress?

Delete and reinstall the app to remove local progress data.
