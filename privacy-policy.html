Overview

Tappy Tint is an offline coloring app. We do not collect personal information through the app.

Data We Collect

  • We do not collect personal data such as name, email, phone number, precise location, contacts, photos, or payment details.
  • We do not use third-party analytics SDKs, ads SDKs, or tracking SDKs in the app.

Data Stored on Device

The app stores your coloring progress locally on your device so your work is preserved between sessions.

Tracking

We do not track users across apps or websites and we do not use the App Tracking Transparency framework.

Children's Privacy

Tappy Tint does not knowingly collect personal data from children or adults.

Data Deletion

Because app data is stored locally on your device, you can delete all app data by uninstalling the app. See Data Deletion.
